Scholarships in Delhi

You can study in Delhi on fully funded or partially funded scholarships. Government of Delhi and Universities in Delhi offer scholarships. We have listed here some best PhD Scholarships in Delhi, Masters Scholarships in Delhi, and undergraduate level scholarships.

Nishkam-SHDF Scholarship for Professional Courses

  • Delhi, Punjab, and neighbouring regions
  • Up to INR 33,000 per year
  • Engineering, Medical, Technical Courses
  • Diploma/Degree
  • 60% marks in the Class 10 and 12 Examinations
Learn More

SHDF-Nishkam Sikh Welfare Council, New Delhi invites Applications for Scholarships from regular students studying in recognized Colleges and Universities for Professional Courses applicants/Pursuing. This scholarship for appeared in Class X & XII Examination in March 2020 and want to pursue Diploma/Degree Courses during 2020-21. The selected scholars will receive a scholarship Up to INR 33,000 per year per student.

BYPL SASHAKT Scholarship

  • Delhi
  • Up to INR 40,000
  • Any Stream
  • Undergraduate
  • 55% marks in their last appeared examination
Learn More

BYPL SASHAKT Scholarship programme is an initiative of BSES Yamuna Power Limited to support higher education of students coming from underprivileged sections of the society. Under this scholarship programme, the students studying in the final year of their undergraduate programme (any stream) in government institutes in Delhi will be awarded with a financial assistance of up to INR 40,000 to continue their studies and achieve their dreams.

Merit Scholarship for SC/ST/OBC/Minority Students Pursuing Professional/Technical Courses, Delhi

  • Delhi
  • Upto INR 2000 per Month
  • Professional/Technical Courses
  • Undergraduate/Postgraduate level
  • Belong to SC/ST/OBC/Minority community
Learn More

Department for the Welfare of SC/ST/OBC, Government of NCT of Delhi invites applications for Merit Scholarship for SC/ST/OBC/Minority Students Pursuing Professional/Technical Courses, Delhi 2021 from recognised colleges/professional/technical institutions. This scholarship aims to provide financial assistance to eligible students so that they can complete their higher education. Selected students will receive up to INR 1,860 per month for 12 months.
